1. Who We Are

VoomComix is an online platform for original manga, comics and webnovels, owned and operated by Mr. Imtiyaz Sheikh, founded in India in 2024. When this policy refers to "VoomComix", "we", "us" or "our", it means this platform and its operator.

Contact: help@voomcomix.in

2. Data We Collect

Account data

  • Full name and username
  • Email address
  • Gender and age (optional)
  • Preferred language
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Usage data

  • Books and chapters you read
  • Books you follow, like or review
  • Search queries on the Platform
  • Device type, browser and approximate location (country level)
  • Time spent on pages and chapters

Writer-specific data

  • Books, chapters and content you publish
  • Performance analytics — views, followers, reviews per book
  • Bank account details (collected only after monetization approval)
  • Monetization application data

Contract applicant data

  • Full name, email and organisation name
  • Details of the content contract application

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and provide the VoomComix platform and all its features.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To personalise your reading experience and content recommendations.
  • To process writer monetization applications and calculate earnings.
  • To transfer monthly payments to approved writers' bank accounts.
  • To display performance analytics to writers in their dashboard.
  • To respond to support queries sent to our help email.
  • To send important platform updates and announcements.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, spam and policy violations.
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns and feedback.

We do not use your data to serve targeted advertising on VoomComix. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Storage

VoomComix uses Supabase as its database and backend infrastructure. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase is hosted on AWS infrastructure and complies with industry-standard security practices.

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

Book covers and manga/comic page images are stored in Supabase Storage (cloud object storage).

5. Cookies

VoomComix uses essential cookies and browser local storage to:

  • Maintain your login session.
  • Remember your reading preferences (font size, background theme).
  • Store your recently read chapter position.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings, but this will log you out of your account.

6. Third Party Services

VoomComix uses the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database, authentication and file storage.
  • Google Fonts — font rendering (no personal data shared).
  • Google AdSense — advertising on monetized book pages. AdSense may set its own cookies. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • GitHub / Vercel — platform hosting and deployment.

We do not share your personal data with any of these services beyond what is necessary for them to function.

7. Financial Data

Bank account details are collected only from writers who have been approved for monetization. This data is:

  • Stored securely in our database with restricted access.
  • Used exclusively to process monthly earnings transfers.
  • Never shared with any third party other than what is required for the bank transfer process.
  • Never used for any other purpose.

VoomComix does not store any credit card, debit card or payment instrument data.

8. Children's Privacy

VoomComix requires users to be at least 13 years of age to create an account. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at help@voomcomix.in and we will promptly delete the account and associated data.

Access to 18+ content on the platform requires the user to confirm they are 18 or older.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view your account data at any time in your profile settings.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information at any time from your account settings.
  • Data portability: You can request a copy of your personal data by emailing us.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data. Note that published content (books and chapters) may be retained to preserve platform integrity, as described in our Terms & Conditions.
  • Objection: You can object to certain uses of your data by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, email: help@voomcomix.in

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal profile data within 30 days.

Published content (books, chapters, reviews) may be retained even after account deletion to maintain platform content integrity and protect the reading history of followers. This content will be anonymised upon account deletion.

Earnings and payment records are retained for a minimum of 5 years for financial and legal compliance purposes.

11. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement the following measures:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Database access is restricted using row-level security policies.
  • Sensitive data such as bank account details is accessible only to authorised administrators.
  • We regularly review and update our security practices.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system is completely immune to security risks. If you suspect any unauthorised access to your account, change your password immediately and contact us.

12. Contact

For any questions, requests or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled:

  • Email: help@voomcomix.in
  • Contract / legal: contract@voomcomix.in
