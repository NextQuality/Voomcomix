4. Content Policy

All content published on VoomComix must comply with the following rules. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension or permanent ban.

Strictly prohibited content

  • Plagiarism: Publishing content copied from any book, website or other source without authorisation. This results in immediate permanent ban.
  • AI-generated stories: Using AI tools to write the story itself. AI may only be used for grammar and language correction, up to a maximum of 20% of total content.
  • Sexual content involving minors: Any such content results in immediate permanent ban and will be reported to relevant authorities.
  • Hate speech: Content targeting any individual or group based on religion, caste, race, gender, sexuality or disability.
  • Violence without context: Graphic violence that serves no narrative purpose and appears designed solely for shock or harm.
  • Misinformation: Content presenting false information as fact in a way designed to mislead readers.
  • Spam: Repetitive low-quality content posted to game the system or artificially inflate metrics.

Permitted content

  • Mature (18+) content is permitted provided the book is correctly labelled as 18+ at the time of creation.
  • Dark themes such as violence, addiction or trauma are permitted when handled responsibly within the narrative.
  • All Indian languages are permitted.

5. Monetization

Writers whose books meet the following criteria may apply for AdSense revenue monetization through VoomComix.

Eligibility requirements

  • Minimum 5,000 followers on the book.
  • Minimum 20,000 total chapter views on the book.
  • Minimum 50 positive reviews (4 or 5 stars) on the book.
  • At least 80% of the content must be original writing by the author.
  • AI usage must not exceed 20% of total content, and only for grammar corrections.
  • The story must be exclusive to VoomComix and not published on any other platform.

Review process

Once you apply, VoomComix will review your book within 7–14 working days to verify originality and compliance. If the book is rejected, you will receive a written reason and will have up to 3 opportunities to improve and reapply. If the book is approved after 3 rejections based on effort and quality, it may still be approved at VoomComix's discretion.

Revenue and payments

  • Approved writers earn 60% of the AdSense revenue generated by their book's pages.
  • For future book sales, YouTube adaptations or other platform sales, the writer earns 70% of the total revenue.
  • Payments are processed monthly. The minimum transfer threshold is $10 USD. Amounts below this threshold remain pending until the threshold is reached.
  • Payments are transferred directly to the writer's bank account on file.
  • Bank details can only be added once a book has been approved for monetization.
  • VoomComix does not guarantee a specific revenue amount. Revenue depends entirely on AdSense performance.
