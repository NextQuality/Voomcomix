Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before you start writing.
Is it free to publish on VoomComix?+
Yes, completely free. Creating an account, publishing a book, posting chapters — all of it is free. You only earn, you never pay.
Can I publish in Hindi or other Indian languages?+
Yes. VoomComix supports all major Indian languages including Hindi, Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia and Urdu.
Can I use AI to help write my story?+
You can use AI for grammar corrections only — a maximum of 20% of the total content. If the majority of the story is AI-generated, your book will not qualify for monetization and may be removed from the platform.
Can I delete my book after publishing?+
No. Once a book is published it cannot be deleted, to protect readers who have followed and read your chapters. You can edit and rewrite chapters at any time.
How do I schedule chapter releases?+
In the chapter editor, set a date and time in the Schedule field before clicking Publish. The chapter will go live automatically. You can change the schedule before it publishes.
When do I get paid?+
Payments are processed monthly. Your balance needs to reach a minimum of $10 USD before it is transferred to your bank account. The exact amount is set by VoomComix based on AdSense revenue from your book's pages.
What happens if my book is found to be plagiarised?+
If VoomComix verifies that your story is copied from another source, your account will be permanently banned without warning. We take originality extremely seriously.
Can I publish on other platforms too?+
For monetization eligibility, your story must be exclusive to VoomComix. Publishing the same story elsewhere disqualifies you from AdSense revenue on VoomComix.
