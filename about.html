ABOUT VOOMCOMIX

VoomComix is an independent creative platform built on one simple belief — great stories should be free to read.

Born from a passion for storytelling, VoomComix brings original webnovels to readers in Hindi and English — raw, real, and written without a filter.

Every story on VoomComix is 100% original — personally written, edited, and published. No AI-generated content. Real stories. Real emotions.

WHO IS BEHIND VOOMCOMIX?

Mr. Imtiyaz Sheikh is the sole creator, writer, and owner of VoomComix. Every story you read on this platform — from concept to final chapter — is crafted personally by him.

His vision: a space where original Indian storytelling gets the spotlight it deserves. Stories that are bold, culturally rooted, and completely free for readers everywhere.

To build a home for original stories — without paywalls, without subscriptions, without ads. A platform where the story always comes first.
