About VoomComix

India's original platform for manga, comics and webnovels — built to give Indian writers a stage and Indian readers a home.

I
Mr. Imtiyaz Sheikh
Founder & Owner

Built by One Passion

VoomComix was founded in 2024 by Mr. Imtiyaz Sheikh as a solo passion project — born out of a simple belief that India deserves its own dedicated platform for original manga, comics and novels written in Indian languages by Indian writers.

What started as a single-person vision has grown into a platform that connects original storytellers with readers across India. Every feature, every rule and every design decision on VoomComix reflects a commitment to originality, creativity and fair rewards for writers.

VoomComix is 100% free for readers — always. And for writers, it offers a transparent path to earning real revenue from the stories they pour their hearts into.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and loved platform for original storytelling — a place where every Indian writer, regardless of language or background, can share their imagination with the world and build a sustainable creative career.

We envision a future where Indian manga, comics and webnovels are read globally — and where the writers behind them are recognized and rewarded fairly for their work.

Our Mission

To provide a completely free, high-quality reading experience for every reader — while giving writers the tools, platform and revenue they deserve for creating original content.

Our mission is to protect originality, celebrate creativity and build a community where great stories rise to the top — not based on who can pay the most, but based on the quality of the writing.
